What is kendo

Kendo (剣道 the way of the sword) is a modern Japanese martial art derived from kenjutsu which is japanese swordmenship. Kendo uses bamboo sword and protective armour allowing it to be a semi contact activity. It is mostly practiced within japan being the most popular traditional martial art. It is a part of PE curriculum which contributes to its popularity. It is estimated there are about 1.7 mln practitioners in Japan and 6 mln world wide.

Kendo concept

Kendo is a way to discipline the human character through the application of the principles of the katana.
